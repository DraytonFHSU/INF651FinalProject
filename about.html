Hello! This is my project for my Front-end Web Development class. It's a semi-spooky take on clicker games like cookie clicker and such.
Hopefully it's nifty!

As for Easter Eggs, I'll let you know that you can click on the light switch and the basement door while the animations are going off, and that means you can get a really big number for the high score. My Highest is 1317.
